Delete Account Instructions

How to Delete Your Account

To delete your account, email us at info@homebooks.app with the subject “Delete Account Request” from the email address associated with your account.

We’ll verify your request via a brief reply. After verification, deletion is performed immediately.

What Will Be Deleted

  • Account details (email, display name, profile photo)
  • Library data (books you added and related metadata)
  • Notes and text entries
  • Images/photos attached to books
  • Audio notes/recordings
  • Any other content stored in your account

What We Retain

None. Once your account is deleted, we do not retain your personal data or content. Cloud data associated with your account is deleted immediately after account deletion.

Timeline

  • Verification: We verify your request via email.
  • Deletion: Your account and all associated data are deleted immediately after verification.

Questions

If you have any questions before or after deletion, contact us at info@homebooks.app.
