Privacy Policy

Effective date: September 6, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains what information Home Books ("we", "us", or "our") collects, how we use it, and your rights. This policy applies to our iOS and Android apps and any website or page where this policy is posted.

By using the app, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

Personal information you provide

  • Account details: email, display name, profile photo (if you sign in with Google or Apple).
  • Content you add: book details, notes, images, audio notes, and other inputs you choose to store.

Device and app information

  • Basic diagnostics and performance data generated by the app (e.g., error logs), strictly for troubleshooting and improving stability.
  • We also use analytics tools (e.g., Firebase Analytics) to understand usage patterns and improve the app.

Media and files (with permission)

  • Camera and photos: used to scan barcodes or add book cover images.
  • Microphone: used to record audio notes (if you use this feature).
  • Local files: images or documents you choose to import into the app.

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and maintain the app, including syncing your library across devices (if signed in).
  • Create and manage your account and authentication sessions.
  • Store your content (books, images, audio, notes) locally and/or in the cloud, based on how you use the app.
  • Improve reliability, performance, and user experience (e.g., diagnostics, crash resolution, analytics).
  • Send optional in-app reminders/notifications you configure.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not show third-party ads or use advertising SDKs.

Where Your Data Lives

On-device storage

We store data locally on your device for speed and offline access. You can delete local data by clearing the app’s data or uninstalling the app.

Cloud storage (if you sign in)

We use secure cloud services to sync and back up your content. Cloud data is associated with your account and is deleted immediately when you delete your account. We do not retain your data after account deletion.

Sharing and Disclosure

We share data only as needed to run the app and as required by law:

  • Service providers: We use reputable providers (e.g., Firebase) to host data, authenticate users, deliver core functionality, and analyze app usage.
  • Legal compliance: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights, users, or the public.
  • No sale of personal data: We do not sell your personal information.

Permissions (Android and iOS)

  • Camera: scanning barcodes, capturing book covers.
  • Photos/Media/Library: attaching images to books.
  • Microphone: recording audio notes.
  • Notifications: delivering reminders you configure.

You can grant or revoke these permissions in your device settings. Some features may not work without the corresponding permission.

Data Retention

  • Account data and cloud content: kept while your account is active and deleted immediately once you request account deletion. We do not retain this data afterward.
  • Local device data: remains until you delete it (e.g., via app settings, OS app storage settings, or by uninstalling the app).
  • Backups and logs: retained for a limited period for reliability and security, then deleted or anonymized.

Security

We use industry-standard safeguards to protect your data:

  • Encryption in transit (TLS) between the app and our cloud services.
  • Provider-level encryption at rest for cloud data (e.g., Firebase).
  • Access controls and rules to ensure only authorized access.

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; we work to continuously improve protections.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data.
  • Port your data to another service.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing.
  • Withdraw consent for optional features (e.g., permissions) at any time.

Requests: Use any in-app account tools (if available) or contact us at info@homebooks.app. We may ask to verify your identity before acting on requests.

Children’s Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your region). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child provided us data, contact us to delete it.

International Data Transfers

Our service providers may process data in the United States and other countries. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards (such as standard contractual clauses) to protect your information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post the updated policy with a new “Effective date.” Material changes will be highlighted within the app or on our website when feasible.

Contact Us

Questions or requests about privacy: Home Books, info@homebooks.app

Managing Your Data

Export

Contact us to request an export of your account data. If self-service export becomes available in the app, you can use it directly.

Deletion

  • Local: remove app data via app settings or by uninstalling the app.
  • Cloud: request account deletion or use any in-app delete controls (if available). Cloud data is deleted immediately after account deletion.

For step-by-step instructions, see Delete Account Instructions.

Permissions

Revoke camera, microphone, photos, and notifications permissions in device settings. Some features will be limited without these permissions.
